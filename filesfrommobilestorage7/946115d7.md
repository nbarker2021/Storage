**Problem:** Φ(x,t,d) is described ("informational potential") but never given explicit form

**Impact:** Cannot derive predictions, cannot test, cannot publish

**Fix:** Provide differential equation:
$$\partial_t \Phi + V(\Phi, \nabla\Phi) = -\sigma(\Phi)$$

With boundary conditions and coupling to Shannon entropy explicitly stated

**Estimated effort:** 1-2 weeks

### Issue 2: Conservation Law Unification Is Assertion, Not Derivation

**Problem:** Paper claims ΔE = ΔH = ΔQ but they have different units

**Impact:** Core claim of framework is mathematically incoherent

**Fix:** Prove with explicit coupling constant k that:
$$\Delta E = k_B T \ln(2) \cdot k \cdot \Delta H_{\text{Shannon}}$$
$$\Delta Q = \Delta E / T$$

Show this follows from single PDE

**Estimated effort:** 2-3 weeks

**Recommendation:** Shelve for now. Get Papers 1-3 solid first.

**Estimated effort:** Decision + reframing if (A) or (B)

**Problem:** Paper 3's main validation (63.2% boundedness) reported without error bars, significance test, or confidence intervals
