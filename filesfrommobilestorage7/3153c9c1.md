### Phase 2: Momentum-Biased Modulation

**Method:** Added **inward/outward particle classification** based on distance from centroid. Applied temporal phase-switching:
- **t ∈ [0, 0.25]:** Boost inward particles (1.5×), suppress outward (0.7×)
- **t ∈ [0.25, 0.5]:** Boost outward, suppress inward
- **t ∈ [0.5, 0.75]:** Continue outward boost
- **t ∈ [0.75, 1.0]:** Return to inward boost

**Results:**
```

432 Hz     [7.54, 7.46, 7.46, 7.54]  1.460    1.000      1.011
216 Hz     [7.54, 7.46, 7.46, 7.54]  1.461    0.999      1.011
```

**Finding:** Introduced **slight asymmetry** (7.54 vs 7.46) with perfect palindrome, but IOOI ratio (1.011) remained far from target (1.50). Required stronger depth-dependent bias.
