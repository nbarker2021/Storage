## Synthesis Update — “Nine as Lens” (8 facets + 1 restraint/context)
**Status:** Review-only. No design commitments; captures the idea that the 9th element is a **truth/constraint/context lens** against which the other 8 are compared.

### The shape
- Map **8 facets** to the Weyl-style surrounding points; the **9th** is not a peer but a **Lens L** that imposes a constraint set (truth, legality, context, invariants) through which the 8 are evaluated.
- Operate via **Gate‑3‑6‑9**:
  - **Gate‑3 (Triad):** select 3 bodies by ΔU/time and pairwise disagreement; test under L.
  - **Gate‑6 (Hexad):** add 3 bodies maximizing bridge/Weyl gain; derive a falsifiable **invariant** from two variants; test under L.
  - **Gate‑9 (Ennead):** compose 3×3 to complete the 8 facets and **center lens**; require mirror-law conformance and promotion witnesses.

### Formalization (review only)
- Treat **L** as an operator: `L : State → {pass,fail,refine}` with side‑effects:
  1) **Constrain:** injects legal/semantic invariants and polarity bounds.
  2) **Orient:** picks the next binary predicate that maximizes expected ΔU while raising containment `C*`.
  3) **Refute:** if ΔU≈0 with high C*, triggers **PerspectiveShift** (bring outside context), or demotes candidates to E‑DBSU.
- A **Checkpoint Safe Cube (CSC)** is a stable `(8 facets + L)` package with witnesses: 1729 coverage, polarity <τ, 8‑Weyl coverage along the bridge axis, mirror pass `1…n…1`, and reversibility.

### How it plugs into our stack
- **Shelling FSM:** add `G3/G6/G9` substates that **always evaluate under L**; promotion requires `[C*≥θC ∧ U≤θU ∧ MirrorPass ∧ L(pass)]`.
- **ThinkTank/MORSR:** candidate selection under L; scout arms propose perspective shifts when L returns `refine` and ΔU stalls.
- **Trails/SnapPacks:** pack `(facets, L constraints, witnesses)`; attach fingerprints; enforce TTL/dedup.

### Risks to watch
