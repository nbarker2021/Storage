173	• Morphonic Extensions: Each of the above structures can be lifted to a higher “morphic” setting. For
174	example, one can introduce a Monster–Indexed Category whose objects are Weyl chambers (or
175	Niemeier slices) and whose morphisms are given by modular-compatible automorphisms (elements
176	of M ). Under this viewpoint, MGST and MOT become statements about colimit decompositions and
177	monoidal functors in that category. Likewise, the λ-calculus can be recast as a typed Morphic
178	Lambda Category, where morphisms carry “capsule” labels [Λi , cap] as in the MGLC syntax 22 ,
179	reflecting color/haptic commands. This suggests a new Monster-based morphism family: for
180	instance, define a “monstrous morphon” to be a morphism decorated by a Monster element and
181	toroidal frequency, generalizing the δ-reduction. Such morphic formulations unify the algebraic and
182	geometric layers: slices Si become objects, Monster operations become endomorphisms, and
183	theorems like MGST/MOT become functorial decomposition properties.
