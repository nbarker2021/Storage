# CQE Glossary of Terms, Notation, and Algebraic Expressions

**Authors:** CQE Research Consortium  
**Document Type:** Glossary Reference  
**Version:** 1.0

This glossary collects all key terms, symbols, and algebraic expressions used throughout the CQE/MORSR paper suite for quick reference.

## 1. Core Mathematical Structures

Term | Symbol | Definition
---|---|---
Cartan Subalgebra | \(\mathfrak{h}\) | The 8-dimensional maximal abelian subalgebra of the exceptional Lie algebra \(\mathfrak{e}_8\).
CQE Embedding | \(\phi: \mathcal{D} \to \mathfrak{h}\) | Function embedding any domain object into \(\mathfrak{h}\).
E₈ Lattice | \(\Lambda_{E_8}\) | Even, unimodular lattice of rank 8 with optimal sphere packing.
Weyl Group | \(\mathcal{W}\) | Group generated by reflections in the root system of \(\mathfrak{e}_8\).
D₈ Dihedral Group | \(D_8\) | Symmetry group of the 8-gon, includes 8 rotations and 8 reflections.

## 2. Vectors and Coordinates

Term | Symbol | Definition
---|---|---
Cartan Vector | \(\mathbf{v} = (v_1, \ldots, v_8)\) | Coordinates of an embedding in \(\mathfrak{h}\).
Root System | \(\mathbf{R} = \{\alpha_1, \ldots, \alpha_{240}\}\) | Set of minimal nonzero vectors in \(\Lambda_{E_8}\).
Simple Roots | \(\Delta = \{\alpha_1, \ldots, \alpha_8\}\) | Basis for the root system.
Policy Channels | \(c_i(\mathbf{v})\) | Coefficients of \(\mathbf{v}\) in the 8 real harmonic basis under D₈.
Harmonic Basis | \(\{
\mathbf{e}_i\}_{i=1}^8\) | Orthonormal basis: DC, Nyquist, and 3 cosine-sine pairs.

## 3. Objective Function and Components

Term | Symbol | Expression
---|---|---
CQE Objective | \(\Phi(\mathbf{v})\) | \(\sum_{i=1}^7 w_i(t) \phi_i(\mathbf{v})\)
Coxeter Penalty | \(\phi_1\) | \(\|\mathbf{P}_{\text{Cox}}\mathbf{v}\|^2 + \lambda\sum_{i}(v_{i+1}-v_i)^2\)
ECC Syndrome  | \(\phi_2\) | \(\alpha S_{\text{Ham}}(\mathbf{v}) + (1-\alpha)S_{\text{Golay}}(\mathbf{v})\)
Sparsity Penalty | \(\phi_3\) | \(\lambda_1\|\mathbf{v}\|_1 + \lambda_2\sum_g\|\mathbf{v}_g\|_2\)
Kissing Number  | \(\phi_4\) | \(|\{\alpha: \|\mathbf{v}-\alpha\|\le\tau\}| - 240\)
Lattice Coherence | \(\phi_5\) | \(\min_{p\in\Lambda_{E_8}}\|\mathbf{v}-p\|^2\)
Channel Deviation | \(\phi_6\) | \(\sum_{i=1}^8|c_i(\mathbf{v})-c_i^{\text{target}}|^2\)
Domain Penalty  | \(\phi_7\) | Domain-specific (e.g., palindrome, spectral decay)

## 4. MORSR Protocol Notation

Term | Symbol | Definition
---|---|---
MORSR State | \(S=(\mathbf{v},\mathcal{L},\mathcal{E},t)\) | Embedding, lane saturations, escrow set, iteration count
Lane Saturation | \(L_i\) | Success rate and gradient decay measure for lane \(i\)
Escrow Policy | \(\mathcal{E}\) | Set of temporarily rejected candidate states with timeouts
Pulse Tolerance | \(\epsilon_{\text{tolerance}}(t)\) | \(\epsilon_0e^{-t/\tau}\): controlled relaxation
Normal Cone | \(\mathcal{N}_{\mathcal{C}}(\mathbf{v})\) | Set of normals to feasible region at \(\mathbf{v}\)

## 5. Constraints and Certificates

Term | Symbol | Expression
---|---|---
Inequality Constraints | \(g_i(\mathbf{v})\le0\) | Domain or channel bounds
Equality Constraints  | \(h_j(\mathbf{v})=0\) | Symmetry or structural constraints
KKT Stationarity | \(\nabla\Phi + \sum\lambda_i\nabla g_i+\sum\nu_j\nabla h_j \in \mathcal{N}_{\mathcal{C}}\)
Complementary Slackness | \(\lambda_i g_i=0\) | Active inequality conditions
Dual Feasibility     | \(\lambda_i\ge0\) | Non-negativity of multipliers
Primal Feasibility   | \(g_i\le0,\,h_j=0\) | Constraints satisfied at solution
