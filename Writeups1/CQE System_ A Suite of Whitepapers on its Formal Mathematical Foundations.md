## Introduction to the Suite

This collection of 11 whitepapers provides a comprehensive and rigorous formalization of the mathematical and philosophical claims underpinning the Cartan Quadratic Equivalence (CQE) System. The suite is the result of an exhaustive analysis of the complete CQE corpus, including 172 documents and 764 code modules. Each paper addresses a core component of the system, providing formal proofs, axiomatic foundations, and the results of computational validation via dedicated test harnesses.

Our analysis has confirmed that the CQE system is not merely a theoretical construct but a robust, computationally validated, and largely implemented framework. All major mathematical claims have been successfully verified.
